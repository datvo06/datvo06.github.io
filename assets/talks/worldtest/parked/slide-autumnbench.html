AutumnBench: WorldTest made concrete

The kitchen test, formalized: Predict becomes Masked Frame Prediction, Adapt becomes Change Detection, Plan becomes Planning.

?
43 environments
Interactive grid worlds written in Autumn: grids 3x3 to 25x25, up to 5 object types each, 19 of 43 stochastic. The same worlds are playable by humans in the browser and by AI as text. Each is a POMDP, the most general environment type.
?
Masked Frame Prediction
Predict the masked content of the final frame by choosing one of six options. Tests prediction of hidden state. The kitchen's Predict.
?
Planning
Drive a subgrid into a target configuration with an action sequence. Tests long-horizon control and reachability. The kitchen's Plan.
?
Change Detection
One rule changes mid-test. Report the earliest timestep at which it changed. Tests detecting shifts in dynamics. The kitchen's Adapt.
43 environments × 3 task families = 129 tasks
