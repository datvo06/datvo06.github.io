ESC, a training-free self-correction framework for vision-language models, is accepted at ECCV'26 (main track). A verifier flags a likely-wrong initial response, then emotional feedback triggers the model to reflect and revise, improving reliability across safety, hallucination, perception, and reasoning benchmarks without any post-training. [<i class="ai ai-arxiv"></i>arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02089), [<i class="fas fa-flask"></i>project](https://genai4e.github.io/ESC/).
