WorldTest is a protocol for evaluating world-model learners that separates reward-free interaction from a scored test in a related environment. We instantiate it as AutumnBench: 43 grid-worlds and 129 tasks spanning masked-frame prediction, planning, and causal-dynamics prediction. Accepted at ICML'26; Archana and I will be presenting, see you in Korea! Across 517 humans and three frontier models we tested, humans win. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.19788), [project](https://www.basis.ai/blog/autumn-platform-2025/).
